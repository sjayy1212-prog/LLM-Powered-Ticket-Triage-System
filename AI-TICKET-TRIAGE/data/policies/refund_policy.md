# Refund Policy

- Full refunds are available within 30 days of purchase.
- After 30 days, only partial refunds (prorated) are offered.
- Refunds for annual plans require manager approval.
- Duplicate charges should be refunded immediately with no questions asked.
- Refund processing time: 3-5 business days for credit cards, 5-10 for bank transfers.
- If a customer has received a refund in the past 90 days, escalate to a supervisor.
