# Fraud Policy

- All fraud reports must be escalated to the Risk and Security team immediately.
- Temporarily lock the payment method associated with the reported transaction.
- Do not disclose investigation details to the customer.
- Acknowledge the report and provide a case reference number.
- Target resolution time: 24 hours for initial investigation, 72 hours for full resolution.
- If the disputed amount exceeds $500, escalate to senior fraud analyst.
